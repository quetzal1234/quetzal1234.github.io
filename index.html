This data set comes from figshare. It is data deposited in the figshare repository from a scholarly article. The dataset has three columns: the year, the numbe of pages of the ikea catalog from that year, and the url for the catalog from that year on ikea’s website. I used a line plot with point marks (so, in other words, a connected scatter plot) to try to show the trend over time in the number of pages (generally, it trends upward and more pages are added over time).
